Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy is a safe, non-invasive treatment where patients breathe pure oxygen in a pressurized chamber. This allows oxygen to reach areas of the body that may be starved of it, supporting healing and recovery at the cellular level.

HBOT is used to support recovery from sports injuries, concussions, chronic wounds, post-surgical healing, inflammation, and brain-related conditions. Many patients also use it to enhance overall wellness, energy, and longevity.
